Composition: Milk is a nutrient-rich liquid composed of water, fats, proteins, lactose (milk sugar), vitamins, and minerals. Types: The most common types of milk come from cows, but milk can also be obtained from other mammals like goats, sheep, and buffalo. Plant-based alternatives such as almond, soy, and oat milk are also popular. Nutritional Value: Milk is a good source of calcium, vitamin D, vitamin B12, potassium, and other nutrients essential for bone health and overall well-being.
