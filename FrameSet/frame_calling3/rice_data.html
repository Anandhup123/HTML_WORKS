Scientific Name: Oryza sativa (Asian rice) and Oryza glaberrima (African rice). Origin: Rice is believed to have been cultivated in China over 10,000 years ago. Types: There are thousands of varieties of rice, but they are commonly categorized into long-grain, medium-grain, and short-grain types. Nutritional Value: Rice is a good source of carbohydrates and provides essential nutrients such as iron, thiamine, and niacin. However, it is low in fiber and protein compared to other grains.
