BABY PRODUCTS

ABOUT :

Baby powder is a talcum powder used to prevent diaper rash, as a deodorant, and for other cosmetic uses. It is typically made from talc, a mineral that is composed of magnesium, silicon, and oxygen When using baby powder, it's important to apply it carefully to avoid inhalation. Shake a small amount of powder into your hand and then apply it to the desired area, gently rubbing it into the skin. There have been concerns about the safety of talcum powder due to its potential link to ovarian cancer and respiratory issues. Inhaling talcum powder can cause respiratory problems, especially in infants
