A camera is a device used to capture and record images. It consists of a lens that focuses light onto a light-sensitive surface,
such as film or a digital sensor, to create a photograph or video. Cameras come in various types, including digital cameras,
which store images electronically, and film cameras, which use photographic film to record images. Cameras are widely used in photography, filmmaking,
surveillance, and many other applications.
